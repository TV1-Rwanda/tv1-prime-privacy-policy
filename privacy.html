TV1 Prime Privacy Policy

This privacy policy (“Policy”) describes how TV1 Prime (“TV1 Prime”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application (the “App”). This privacy policy was last updated on 20th June 2023

Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information from you:

  • User information & contacts: We may collect user information such as names, emails, telephone numbers, addresses, etc
  • Usage information: We collect information about how you use the App, such as the videos you watch, and the time you spend using the App.
  • Payment information: If you purchase a subscription to TV1 Prime, we will collect your payment information, such as your credit card number or PayPal account information.

How do we collect your Personal information

We collect your information in the following two ways:

  • Information you provide to us: You may provide your personal information voluntarily while creating your account, or when you contact us or make payments. You may also give information about the category of content you prefer by liking and bookmarking content.
  • Information collected automatically: While using the app, usage information is recorded automatically.

Why do We Use Your Personal Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with the App: We use your personal information to create an account you use to access the App’s content and features.
  • To improve the App: We use your personal information to improve the App and its features.
  • To personalize your experience: We use your personal information to personalize your experience with the App, such as by recommending videos we think you might like.
  • To communicate with you: We may use your personal information to share with you about the App, such as sending you notifications about new features or content.
  • To comply with the law: We may use your personal information to comply with applicable law, such as to respond to a subpoena or court order.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases where we limit the amount of information shared to only what is necessary:

  • Service providers: We may share your personal information with service providers who help us operate the App, such as payment processors.
  • With your consent: We may share your personal information with other third parties with your consent.

Data Security

We put in place measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Physical security: We store your personal information in secure facilities that comply with modern security standards
  • Technical security: We use encryption and other security measures to protect your personal information when it is transmitted over the internet.
  • Employee training: We train our employees on the importance of protecting your personal information.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to access your personal information.
  • Correction: You have the right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information that we have about you.
  • Deletion: You have the right to delete your personal information.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Portability: You have the right to receive your personal information in a format that is easy to use and share.
  • Withdrawal of consent: You have the right to withdraw your consent to our processing of your personal information at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at tv1development@gmail.com

Retention of Your Personal Data

We will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as your account is active. After six months of inactivity, We will reach out to check if you still use our app. In case you no longer wish to use our service, we will immediately delete the identifiable personal information you provided to us. However, we may keep anonymous data related to your usage of the app.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age since all our users are registered and are required to be above 13 years to get an account. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete their account from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to take the necessary actions.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes to this Policy, we will notify you by email or through a notice on the App.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Policy, please contact us at tv1development@gmail.com
